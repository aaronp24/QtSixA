Connect the keypad



You should know, the keypad only works through bluetooth.

Connecting it over USB charges the internal battery, but doesn't do anything else besides that.

So, if you don't have bluetooth, the keypad won't work.


To use the keypad wirelessly, you'll first have to pair it. Please check the “Connect Sixaxis through bluetooth” section for that


Then turn on the keypad (a little switch on the right), and press any key.

The green and red leds will start blinking, then the red stays on – it connected.

If the keypad doesn't not connect this way (just keeps blinking the 2 leds), try using the force option here too, or wait until it stops blinking, then try running:

sixad --stop

sixad --start

in a terminal and press some key again.


Note that the keypad will not fully work right when it connects.

You'll have to wait about 10 seconds for it to initialize.
