QtSixA and sixad depends on the follwing packages:

BlueZ (+bluez-hcidump) (latest versions are highly recommended)
Python 2.5 or 2.6 (2.6 is recommended)
Python-Qt4 (at least v4.4; v4.5 or later recommended)
Python-DBus
KDEsu/KDEsudo/GKsu


Optional:
xserver-xorg-input-joystick (for Sixaxis profiles)
libnotify-bin/notify-send (for notifications)
xdg-utils/xdg-open (for opening web links)
